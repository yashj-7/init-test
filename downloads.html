We Build to Help

Our software provides you to make your own colour just by mixing three primary colours.

How it Works?

The software contains three primary colours i.e. red(R),green(G) and blue(B) each one of them with their value ranging from minimum to maximum. You can slide out values in slidebar and mix three of them to form new colours.

Ex- Red,Green and Blue mixed in 255 composition forms white colour.
(255,255,255) = white colour
Colours mixed in minimum composition i.e. 0 forms black colour.
(0,0,0) = black colour
